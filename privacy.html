Privacy Policy

BrokenStone Valley · Last updated: 2026-05-04

This privacy policy describes how the personal companion tools used to manage the BrokenStone Valley creator account (YouTube channel @brokenstone.valley and TikTok account @brokenstone.valley) handle data. These tools are operated privately by the account owner for single-creator personal use. They are not offered as a service to third parties.

1. What data is accessed

When the account owner authorizes the tools through TikTok Login Kit, the tools may read the following on behalf of the account owner only:

  • Public profile information of the authorized account (display name, avatar, bio, follower and following counts).
  • Lists of videos uploaded to the authorized account, along with their public metadata and basic statistics (views, likes, comments).
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok for the authorized account.

The tools may also upload videos to the authorized account's drafts/inbox, where the account owner manually reviews and finalizes them inside the TikTok mobile app.

2. What data is NOT accessed

  • No data is collected about other TikTok users, viewers, followers, or commenters beyond what is publicly attached to the account's own content.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or data brokers are used.
  • No content is shared with anyone outside the account owner.

3. Where data is stored

OAuth tokens and any cached metadata are stored locally on the account owner's personal computer, in files restricted to the account owner's user account (file permissions 0600). They are never uploaded to any external service or third-party server.

4. Data retention and deletion

The account owner may revoke the tools' access at any time from tiktok.com/setting/apps. Upon revocation, any stored OAuth tokens become invalid. Local cache files can be deleted by the account owner at any time.

5. Children's privacy

These tools are not intended for use by anyone under 13. The authorized account is operated by an adult.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: brokenstone.valley@gmail.com
